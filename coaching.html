Story Of My Life

My path into this work began when my child's health became critical. The edical system I had trusted could no longer provide the answers we needed, and life suddenly shifted into survival mode. I found myself navigating fear, exhaustion, and the overwhelming responsibility of caring for my child while trying to hold myself together. On the outside, I was functioning. On the inside, I was carrying more than I had words for. In that season of uncertainty, everything slowed down - but everything felt louder internally. I began to notice something deeper happening within me. I wasn't only responding to the crisis, I was being confronted with long-held patterns I had never fully questioned - overthinking, control, emotional suppression, and the need to always be strong. I realized I wasn't just navigating circumstances, I was also navigating myself.

Coaching Options

1:1 PRIVATE COACHING

  • A flexible, ongoing coaching space designed for personalized support as you navigate life in real time.
  • This option is ideal if you are not looking for a structured program, but instead want consistent guidance, clarity, and support as your needs evolve.
  • Together, we focus on what is most present for you - whether that is emotional overwhelm, decision-making, self-doubt, relationships, or feeling stuck in certain areas of your life.
  • Each session is centered around helping you slow down, gain clarity, and reconnect with yourself so you can move forward with more confidence and self-trust.
  • This is a supportive space for reflection, grounding, and real-time coaching as life unfolds.

8 WEEK 1:1 COACHING PROGRAM

  • A structured, high-touch transformation journey designed to move you from overwhelm and self-doubt into clarity, confidence, and aligned action.
  • This is a focused 8-week experience where we work closely together using the Kahal Method to help you understand your internal patterns, release emotional blocks, and rebuild trust in yourself.
  • Each week is intentionally designed to build on the last, guiding you through a clear progression of awareness, emotional release, and practical integration.
  • This program includes:
    • Weekly private 1:1 coaching sessions
    • Between-session support for guidance and integration
    • Personalized worksheets and reflection exercises
    • Structured support to help you apply insights in real life
  • This is not just about gaining clarity in session - it is about creating real, lived change in how you think, choose, and show up in your everyday life.
  • By the end of the 8 weeks, you will have greater emotional awareness, stronger self-trust, and a clearer sense of direction rooted in alignment-not overwhelm.

My Coaching Approach

My coaching is rooted in one core belief: you don't need to be fixed-you need to be reconnected with yourself. Most people try to change their lives by

focusing only on external goals, surface-level motivation, or quick mindset shifts. While those can create temporary movement, they rarely create lasting change. Real transformation happens when you understand what is driving your thoughts, emotions, and patterns beneath the surface.

My coaching is rooted in one core belief: you don't need to be fixed - you need to be reconnected with yourself. Most people try to change their lives by focusing only on external goals, surface-level motivation, or quick mindset shifts. While those can create temporary movement, they rarely create lasting change. Real transformation happens when you understand what is driving your thoughts, emotions, and patterns beneath the surface.

My approach is a blend of structured life coaching and deep internal awareness work. Together, we focus on slowing down the internal noise so you can see yourself clearly again - how you think, how you respond, and what patterns are quietly shaping your life.

From there, we begin to shift them intentionally.

This is not about pushing harder or becoming someone new. It's about building self-awareness, emotional clarity, and self-trust so you can start making decisions from alignment instead of overwhelm.

The foundation of my work is the Kahal Method, which guides this transformation in a simple, grounded process:

  • Understanding yourself more deeply
  • Releasing patterns that keep you stuck
  • Rebuilding trust in your inner voice
  • Taking aligned, intentional action in your life

Each session is designed to create both reflection and forward movement - so insight is never just awareness, it becomes lived change.

My role is to help you slow down, see clearly, and reconnect with the part of you that already knows what you need.

This work is not about quick fixes.
It is about creating a steady internal foundation you can live from.
