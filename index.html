About Me

Hi, I’m Prakruti Patel - a mother, and I guide women ready to come back to themselves.

The Kahal Method is rooted in my personal experience navigating a season of intense family challenges that left me feeling overwhelmed and disconnected from myself. Having walked the path from total self-doubt back to grounded confidence, I understand the weight of losing yourself - and the power of reclaiming your life.

My approach is simple but powerful: We don’t fix you.
We reconnect you.

Because everything you’re searching for already exists within you.
